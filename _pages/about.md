I am Xu Liao, a Ph.D. Candidate in Biostatistics and Health Data Science  at Duke-NUS medical school, National University of Singapore. I obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Statistics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019.

My research is centered on RNA velocity, representation learning, deep generative models, and clustering analysis. I employ advanced statistical techniques to analyze complex biological datasets, including spatial transcriptomics and single-cell RNA-seq data. I excel in applying both deep learning and statistical methods to offer novel perspectives on these datasets. Looking forward, I am interested in AI for science.
