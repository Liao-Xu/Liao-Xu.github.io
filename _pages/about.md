**Xu Liao** is a biostatistics researcher who completed his Ph.D. in Biostatistics and Health Data Science at Duke-NUS Medical School, National University of Singapore in 2024, under the supervision of [Prof. Jin Liu](https://sites.google.com/view/liujinlab/home). He earned his Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019, where he was mentored by [Prof. Yao Wang](https://yaowang15.github.io/).

Her research focuses on RNA velocity, representation learning, deep generative models, and clustering analysis. She employ advanced statistical techniques to model complex biological datasets, particularly in spatially resolved transcriptomics and single-cell RNA sequencing data. Her expertise lies in applying both deep learning and statistical methods to reveal novel insights from these datasets.
