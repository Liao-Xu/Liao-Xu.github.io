I am Xu Liao, a Ph.D. Candidate in Biostatistics and Health Data Science at Duke-NUS medical school, National University of Singapore. I obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Statistics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019.

My research focuses on RNA velocity, representation learning, deep generative models, and clustering analysis. I employ advanced statistical techniques to model complex biological datasets, particularly in spatially resolved transcriptomics and single-cell RNA sequencing data. My expertise lies in applying both deep learning and statistical methods to reveal novel insights from these datasets. Looking forward, I am interested in AI for science.
