Xu Liao is a biostatistics researcher who completed her Ph.D. in Biostatistics and Health Data Science at Duke-NUS Medical School, National University of Singapore in 2024, under the supervision of [Prof. Jin Liu](https://sites.google.com/view/liujinlab/home). She earned her Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019, where she was mentored by [Prof. Yao Wang](https://som.xjtu.edu.cn/en/info/1114/1219.htm).

Her research focuses on RNA velocity, representation learning, deep generative models, and clustering analysis. She employs advanced statistical techniques and deep learning methods to model complex biological datasets, particularly in spatially resolved transcriptomics and single-cell RNA sequencing. Her work aims to reveal novel insights from high-dimensional biological data through deep learning and statistical methods.
